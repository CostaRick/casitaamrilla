While the main room had already been built before, major improvements have been made. The Wall to the ocean has been opened and a huge glass-sliding door has been installed.

A bathroom was added to the main room. The roof is not attached to the base-wall but 3 meters higher above the ground, giving the experience of have an outdoor shower while staying inside. Modern equipment, daylight and unlimited fresh air make this bathroom unique.
